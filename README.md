# 📊 Smoking-Health-Report: Data Analysis and Health Indicators

[![Python](https://img.shields.io/badge/Python-3.12+-blue.svg)](https://www.python.org/)
[![Pandas](https://img.shields.io/badge/Pandas-Data%20Analysis-150458.svg)](https://pandas.pydata.org/)

**Smoking-Health-Report** is an Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) project focused on identifying health patterns, medical costs, and correlations between lifestyle and biometric indicators. The goal is to transform raw data into visual and actionable insights for health management.

---

## 🚀 Features and Insights

### 🔍 Data Science Analysis
* **Age Distribution of Smokers:** Identification of uniform sampling patterns in health datasets.
* **Financial Impact of Smoking:** Comparative analysis of medical expenses, revealing that smokers can have costs up to 4x higher than non-smokers.
* **BMI vs. Costs Correlation:** Study of the relationship between Body Mass Index (BMI) and medical expenses using Pearson correlation coefficients.
* **Outlier Handling:** Identification and analysis of extreme BMI cases above and below critical thresholds.

### 💻 Interface and Visualization
* **Interactive Dashboard:** Visualization of bar charts, boxplots, and correlation heatmaps.
* **Vite Environment:** A fast and modern frontend for presenting health reports.

---

## 🛠️ Technologies Used

### Data Science
- **Python**: Main language for processing.
- **Pandas**: DataFrame manipulation and cleaning.
- **Matplotlib, Seaborn & Plotly**: Generation of statistical visualizations and high-quality charts. Obs: Plotly is a interactive graph, pretty good for presentations
- **Jupyter Notebooks**: Environment for experimentation and analysis documentation.

### Standard Deviation (std) Analysis
